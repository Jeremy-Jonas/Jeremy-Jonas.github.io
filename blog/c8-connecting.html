Complete the political awareness exercise here in writing.

A difficult situation from my past arose in my previous sales job, I was asked to cover breaks for other staff members in different roles which took me away from my role and put me in a position where I was outside of my comfort zone.
From my point of view, I found that the team leader who was making this request of me to leave my role for up to 2 hours every day was making a poor choice and potentially hurting sales as nobody was around to do my role while I was covering breaks.
From the team leader’s point of view, she was using a staff member who appeared to just wander around the shop talking to people to fill a position that really did need to be covered while the regular staff member was taking their break.
